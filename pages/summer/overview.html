Creatures in the Tall Grass

The marsh answers.

Synopsis

A bioacoustics researcher moves to a coastal Connecticut town and picks up a signal from the marsh that doesn’t match wind or weather. He follows it into the reeds and finds an injured creature and a crystal. As the storm rolls in, the hum spreads through the neighborhood—and something in the tall grass closes in.

~90 min• Thriller / Sci‑Fi• Branford, CT• Summer 2026

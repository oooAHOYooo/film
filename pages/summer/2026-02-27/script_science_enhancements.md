# Script Science Enhancements - Full Script Pass

Based on the full plot outline, here is a comprehensive list of grounded scientific concepts and "believable tech" explanations that can be woven into the script to heighten the realism and stakes of the story.

### 1. The Hum, The Birds, and The Battery Drain (Scenes 02, 03)
*   **The Anomalous Hum:** Instead of a generic magical sound, ground the "hum" in physics as **Infrasound** or a **Schumann Resonance anomaly**. 
*   **Bird Magnetoreception:** In Scene 03, the birds are all pointing southeast toward the marsh. You can have Dallas explain (or write in his notes) that birds navigate using **Magnetoreception** via a protein in their eyes called *cryptochrome*. The creatures in the marsh are emitting such a massive, localized magnetic pulse that it has temporarily overpowered the Earth's magnetic pole in Branford, turning the marsh into a "False North." 
*   **Battery Deaths:** The battery dying in Dallas's equipment isn't just "spooky." The creatures emit **EMP-like micro-bursts**. Dallas could realize that traditional batteries die because their chemical reactions are being halted by a highly concentrated magnetic field.

### 2. Biological Tech & The Creature's Physiology (Scenes 09, 11, 12, 18)
*   **Electrogenesis & Piezochromism:** When the creature glows gold and flares the equipment (s11), it's not magic—it's **Electrogenesis** (like an electric eel, but amplifying ambient energy) combined with **Piezochromism** (changing color based on mechanical/acoustic pressure).
*   **Grid Power Drain (s18):** Makayla discovers it's pulling power from the grid at 115.3 MHz. Instead of just saying it's "pulling power," she can specify it's utilizing **Resonant Inductive Coupling**. The creature is acting like an organic Tesla coil, wirelessly siphoning 60Hz alternating current from the Branford power lines through the air. 
*   **Neural Acoustics (s12):** When wires are hooked to the creature's head and it "sings through its back-holes," Dallas could identify that its physiological structure acts as a **Biological Waveguide**. It doesn't use vocal cords; it uses organic electromagnetic resonance chambers to vibrate air molecules directly.

### 3. The Darkness, Trances, and "Ghost Stories" (Scenes 13, 16, 22, 25, 26)
*   **Asher's Trances:** Asher's visions and trances aren't purely psychic; they are **Temporal Lobe Micro-Seizures** induced by specific acoustic frequencies. The creatures emit a frequency that hits the exact resonant frequency of the human amygdala, hacking his neural pathways. 
*   **The "Mother's Vision" & Shadows:** The dark creatures (Red-Eyes/Shadows) use a weaponized form of the "Fear Frequency" (**18.98 Hz**). NASA and acousticians have proven that frequencies around 18.98 Hz resonate perfectly with the human eyeball. It causes extreme feelings of dread, paranoia, and literal smudges in your peripheral vision—creating the illusion of "Shadow People." Sierra's notes (s22) could mention that "Ghost Stories are just localized infrasound poisoning."

### 4. The Climax & The Reverse Device (Scenes 15, 27, 28)
*   **The Plan (s26/s27):** To protect themselves from the Dark Creatures' paralyzing infrasound and electric zaps, Makayla and Dallas don't just use standard armor. They might rig up acoustic **Metamaterial panels** or crude **Faraday Cages** out of copper mesh taken from hardware store screen doors (s03 callback) to ground the electrical attacks.
*   **Light vs Shadow Logic (s28):** The final battle on the field isn't just lasers vs darkness. It's a battle of **Constructive vs Destructive Wave Interference**. The Reverse Device uses the crystal phase-inverter to read the Dark Creatures' attacks and hit them with an **Anti-Wave**. The "Light" creature survives by shifting its own bodily frequency to harmonize with the Reverse Device, creating a **Constructive Resonance** that amplifies the Dome of Silence and obliterates the Shadows' acoustic attacks.

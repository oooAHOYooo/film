# Prop List: Portable Wave Readers (Oscilloscopes)

Research for Dallas’s bioacoustics equipment — handheld/portable oscilloscopes and wave readers. Use for “military grade” receiver, handheld scope, and waveform-on-screen shots.

---

**Prop notes:** Handheld, “flip open” friendly, small screen — good for “Dallas flips open handheld scope and watches waveform.” Dust/duct-tape/sticker treatment will read well.

## Suggested use for Summer

- **Hero handheld:** 2C53T — unpack from box, sticker/duct tape, flip open on trail, waveform ladder, “record” beat.
- **Porch/indoor “big” display:** 1014D — sync with creature heartbeat, dial “to a normal frequency” to put creature to sleep.
- **Optional:** DST-210 as second/backup field unit or for wide shots with multiple devices.

# Omissions Log: Scenes 21 - 27

This file tracks the major concepts, character moments, and plot points that were omitted or significantly altered during the rewrite of the Scene 21-27 climax block. 

## Scene 19 & 22
- **The "Take it Back" Plan:** Originally, the crew gathered in the living room and explicitly discussed a plan to take the creature back to the marsh to return it. This was omitted to favor a more defensive, desperate posture where the Red-Eyes attack the house instead of the characters going on the offensive.
- **Asher's Panic Attack (S22):** Asher originally told Makayla "I don't want to go back." This was removed to keep the momentum driving forward.
- **Dominic's Groceries:** A brief action line of Dominic taking groceries from his minivan was cut.

## Scene 21 & 24
- **Dallas's Tape Recorder:** Dallas originally sat awake at night listening to a tape recording of Sierra explaining "The great hum." This lore exposition was cut to accelerate the appearance of the Red-Eyes.
- **The Glowing Infection:** Dallas originally looked at the burn mark on his arm "and it begins to glow a bit." This was cut because glowing infections felt slightly too magical/fantastical compared to the grounded acoustic tone.
- **Makayla's Burn Marks:** Makayla originally discovered burn marks on her own arm and Dallas's arm during the day. 
- **The Bike Ride:** Makayla and Asher originally rode their bicycles to Dallas's house during the day to check on the burn mark.
- **The Lair Retreat:** The crew originally retreated to Makayla's "Lair" (a tree log by the Long Island Sound) for a quiet philosophical discussion about her mother before the climax. This was entirely cut to compress the timeline and keep the danger centered at the house.

## Scene 23 & 26
- **The Geometric Ring & Extra Crystal:** The golden creatures were originally found inside a geometric ring of grass, centered around a *second* crystal identical to Makayla's. The second crystal element was cut to keep the mechanics simpler.
- **Asher's Telepathic Healing:** Asher originally whined and grabbed his head, and one of the golden elders shook its head to telepathically cure his panic attack. This was slightly modified to the elder emitting an "acoustic hum" to calm his breathing, keeping it grounded in bio-acoustics.
- **The Golden Mist & Paralysis:** The golden elders originally sprayed a "fine mist" that actively paralyzed Dallas and Makayla, forcing them to involuntarily walk backward away from the danger. This explicit bodily control felt too alien/magical and was omitted.
- **Asher's 1-1-5-3 Activation:** In the original marsh confrontation, Asher used a shoulder-rigged oscillator to blast the frequency directly in the swamp. This was moved to the backyard climax.

## Scene 25
- **The Marsh Escape:** Originally, Scene 27 began with them fleeing the marsh after watching the massacre, with Asher having another panic attack in the cold marsh yelling "Run."
- **Makayla's Diagram:** Makayla originally pulled out Sierra's folder during the setup of the trap specifically to point at a "waveform diagram with two opposing frequencies colliding" as an explicit blueprint. 

*(Note: The full, unedited text of original Scenes 21 through 27 has been archived in `original_s21_to_s25.md` in this folder).*

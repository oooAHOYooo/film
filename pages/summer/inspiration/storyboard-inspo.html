Storyboard Inspo

Conceptual look-book generated using Midjourney. Prompting focused on 1980s cinematic film stock, 35mm anamorphic lenses, and deep, atmospheric staging.

Scene 01 • Shot 1 • Medium Wide

Dallas approaches the tall grass edge of his new backyard and makes an unsettling discovery: a makeshift trailcam on a stick hidden among the dense reeds.

Scene 01 • Shot 2 • Medium 2-Shot

Makayla and Asher sit silently on their bicycles in the driveway, watching the new neighbor unpack. Asher ignores the situation, intensely tracing a pattern on his palm.

Scene 07 • Shot 1 • Medium Wide

Dallas stares in amazement at the dusty basement wall, discovering it is covered in strange, esoteric numbers and markings next to the audio workstation.

Scene 07 • Shot 2 • Close Up

Asher ignores the conversation behind him, his eyes fixed intensely on the distant bushes outside. The leaves flutter unnaturally in the pitch black as something unseen moves through them.

Scene 21 • Shot 2 • Close Up Push-In

The "Spielberg Face" — The camera pushes in on Makayla's face as she looks out the window, a strange ethereal glow reflecting in her widened eyes in awe and terror.

Scene 21 • Shot 6 • Extreme Wide Deep Focus

Dallas and Asher stand in the shadowy foreground on the porch. Far across the marsh in extreme deep focus, red eyes pulse in the beating wind.

Scene 26 • Shot 1 • Extreme Wide

The Climax — Thousands of glowing lanterns rise from the tall reeds in the dark, foggy salt marsh at night.

Scene 26 • Shot 2 • Medium Push-In

Dallas and Makayla stand completely frozen in awe and fear, their faces illuminated by the off-screen golden glow of the swarm.

Scene 11 • Shot 3 • Extreme Close Up

Dallas meticulously observes the creature through macro monoculars. A glowing, intricate mark beneath its eye begins to react as his dusty finger approaches.

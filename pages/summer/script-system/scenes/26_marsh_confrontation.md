EXT. SALT MARSH - NIGHT

Dallas and Makayla trek through the reeds, guided by the flickering glow of the crystal in Makayla’s pocket.

They find the injured creature. It is surrounded by others of its kind—smaller, birdlike elders waving their hands.

Suddenly, a mist sprays from the elders' backs. Dallas and Makayla freeze, their bodies paralyzed by the hum. Dozens of golden lights rise like lanterns in the mist, but from the shadows, the red eyes emerge, circling the light.

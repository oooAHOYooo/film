EXT. SALT MARSH - CONTINUOUS

The predators pounce. In a blur of shadow and gnashing sound, the red eyes snatch the golden lights one by one.

Dallas fights the paralysis. He reaches for his frequency dial. He turns it hard—the tiny creature pulses with a blinding light, causing the predators to retract for a split second.

He grabs the creature, but as he pulls it close, the pulsing stops. It’s motionless. The bass scale of the hum drops to a terrifying low. They have to run.

Scene Breakdown

Production-ready breakdown aligned with the script-system (30 scenes) and latest treatment. Each row includes location, time, duration, act, shoot days, key elements, and production notes.

Scene Scene Name Location Time Duration Act Shoot Days Key Elements & Action Production Notes
1 Arrival Dallas' House - Interior Day 3 min Act 1 1 Dallas unpacks. Opens equipment box. Finds military-grade receiver covered in dust. Peels back duct tape revealing photo sticker of Dallas and woman with audio headsets in field. Thumb traces over her face. Presses sticker on receiver. Hears hum. Records readings. Battery dies. Props: Military receiver, duct tape, photo sticker. Sound design: Hum introduction. Emotional beat: Grief/loss subtext.
2 The Hum Town - Street Day 2 min Act 1 0.5 Dallas walks to town for batteries. Meets neighbor Dominic. Dominic follows. Dominic convinces Dallas to attend fellowship lunch at church. Character introduction. Establishing neighborhood. Natural dialogue. Location: Branford streets.
3 Parking Ticket Street - Car Day 2 min Act 1 0.5 Dominic discovers parking ticket. Upset, rants about raccoon problems and where money goes. Dallas notices row of birds on power line - all facing marsh direction. Visual detail: Birds as foreshadowing. Dominic's character development. Practical: Parking ticket prop.
4 Fellowship CPC Church - Fellowship Hall Day - Late Summer 3 min Act 1 1 Fellowship lunch. Circle of churchgoers outside eating Sally's. Last fellowship lunch of summer. Dallas and Dominic participate. Extras needed. Catering: Sally's. Establishing community. Weather: Late summer atmosphere.
5 Marsh Walk Marsh - Walking Path Day 3 min Act 1 1 Dallas and Dominic walk home toward marsh. Wind, New England coast. Coyotes howl in distance. Dialogue: "What are you here for?" "Work." "At Yale?" "Quinnipiac." Dallas explains he has months to settle. "So are you here alone?" "Yep." Sound: Distant coyotes. Location: Coastal marsh path. Character development: Dallas' isolation.
6 News Vans Branford - Marsh Lands Day 2 min Act 1 0.5 They get lost in Branford. Wander through marsh lands and tall grass. Dominic listens to birds, mentions app for species identification. News vans at town center. Pat Clendenen reports on Nor'easter, weather warnings, weekend festival concerns. Location: Multiple marsh locations. News van setup. Weatherman character introduction. Practical: Bird identification app reference.
7 Trash Cans Dominic's House - Exterior Day 1 min Act 1 0.5 Approach house. Dominic upset - trash cans dumped over. Invites Dallas to help clean kids' mess (furniture rearrangement). Practical: Trash cans, furniture. Establishing Dominic's home.
8 The Coordinates Dominic's House - Interior Day 4 min Act 1 1 Dallas notices painting of someone no longer around. Looks at it. Meets eyes with Mr. Mike in corner yelling. Dominic: "That was my wife and my child." Asks if Dallas had someone special. "Yes, but not anymore." Mutual understanding. Mr. Mike scratches coordinates on wooden end table: "45 Left 15 degrees right." Dominic rushes to stop him. Mr. Mike: "She's here" - flustered by weather. Props: Painting, wooden end table. Character: Mr. Mike introduction. Important: Coordinate detail (45 L, 15 R) - plot point.
9 Janice Arrival Dallas' House - Interior Day 3 min Act 1 1 Janice visits with kids. Teases Dallas into dog-sitting Howie. Dallas agrees. Janice discovers Dominic cooking Brussels sprouts incorrectly, gravy a mess. Argument erupts. Janice frantically looks for keys. Makayla (20s) approaches Dallas' equipment, peppers questions. Asher (15) looks at distant bushes, greets Mr. Mike haphazardly. Dominic ushers everyone to car - running late. Whirlwind exit. Dallas alone with Howie. Props: Equipment, keys, Brussels sprouts, gravy. Character introductions: Janice, Makayla, Asher, Howie. Practical: Dog actor/animal handler.
10 Equipment Intro Coastal Trail - Near Tall Grass Day 3 min Act 1 1 Dallas walks home with Howie. Long Island Sound roars against Branford rocks. Portable reader spikes violently near distant field/tall grass. Distant dogs bark, then coyotes. Storm alerts pop up. Hum begins - grows denser, deeper, everywhere. Dallas stares in daze. Howie afraid, prompts Dallas to move on. Sound design: Hum escalation, dogs, coyotes, storm alerts. Visual: Equipment readings. Practical: Dog performance.
11 Howie Walk Dallas' House - Interior Night 2 min Act 1 0.5 Dallas at window, stares at ripping field. Dominic and Asher collect Howie. "Everything okay?" "It is fine." Later: Dallas at window again, equipment ready but silent. Touches photo of late wife. Hum begins. Tall shadows grow in distance, move against wind. Distant sounds become more distinct but distant. Props: Photo of wife. Visual effects: Shadows. Sound: Hum, distant sounds. Lighting: Night interior, exterior shadows.
12 The First Hum Dallas' House - Exterior Dawn 2 min Act 1 0.5 Storm approaches. Janice drops off Howie before museum work (securing artwork). Thanks Dallas. Dallas begins kinship with Howie. Makayla and Asher run to look at something in distance of Dallas' house. Janice yells them to car. Schools canceled due to weather (Janice thinks it's "bull"). Dominic from minivan helps kids in, pets Howie, asks Dallas about Costco and printing songs for life group. Practical: Minivan, Costco boxes. Character: Janice's work at museum. Weather: Storm approaching atmosphere.
13 Shadows in Wind Coastal Trail - Creek Day 3 min Act 2 1 Dallas and Howie on trail. Stop by creek behind house. Roar and hum begin from distance. Howie's ears perk, runs to Dallas to be held. Dallas holds Howie. Light flickers in far distance. Dallas drawn to light, walks toward it in daze (Howie objects). Equipment beeps. Dallas ties Howie to tree at salt marsh edge, enters tall grass. Practical: Tree for tying dog. Visual effects: Distant light flicker. Sound: Roar, hum, equipment beep. Animal handler: Dog performance.
14 The Burn Mark Tall Grass - Tree Hollow Day 2 min Act 2 1 Dallas in daze walks to shrunken tree hollow. Dozens of eyes seem to surround him through tall yellow reeds. Movement of leaves in hollow. Animal cry from movement. Cry continues, puts Dallas in daze as he approaches. Visual effects: Eyes in reeds, creature reveal. Sound: Animal cry. Location: Tall grass, tree hollow. Camera: Daze/subjective POV.
15 Costco Run Tall Grass - Hollow Day 5 min Act 2 2 Dallas approaches shrunken leaf pile. Discovers dying creature inside. Creature breathes heavily. Cry doesn't come from mouth but from flapping holes on back (like instrument). Creature: fragile, birdlike, downy fur, long rabbit-like ears, diminishing flickering glow beneath neck. Dallas amazed, stares at complexity/beauty. Realizes pulse slowing. Dallas touches creature's back - creature quiets, back-holes align with equipment readings. Urgent fear - something massive following, rocketing closer through blades like arrow. Dallas grabs creature in coat, rushes out. Howie freaks out. Journey back to house with creature and Howie. CREATURE REVEAL. Practical effects/VFX: Creature design, glow, back-holes. Sound: Creature cry, equipment sync. Visual effects: Predator approach. Props: Creature puppet/prop, coat. Animal handler: Howie.
16 Entering Grass Dallas' House - Kitchen Day 3 min Act 2 1 Dallas brings creature to kitchen. Outdoor porch begins to sing like instruments - pulse in sync with creature heartbeat. Creature fades. Dallas turns dial to normal frequency - puts creature to sleep. Howie disturbed in corner, waits by door. Dallas tries to hide creature. When creature cries, world outside echoes. Ugly hum responds from far away source. Hum floods town. Windows rattle. Sound design: Porch instruments, heartbeat sync, echo, hum escalation. Practical: Windows rattle effect. Props: Equipment dial, creature.
17 The Hollow Dallas' House - Kitchen Day 2 min Act 2 0.5 Janice and Dominic return with Costco boxes. Dominic thanks Dallas for life group meeting tonight. Dallas tries to avoid them seeing creature. Mr. Mike walks in, freaks out, points at creature: "What is that? What is that?" Makayla spots creature, kneels to attend wound without fear. Creature's glow brightens with her touch. Asher joins huddle. Dominic yells for calm, then notices creature, pushes everyone away. Practical: Costco boxes. Character reactions. Visual effects: Creature glow brightening. Props: Creature, equipment.
18 Creature Rescue Dallas' House - Kitchen Day 2 min Act 2 0.5 Kitchen table - Dallas tries to heal creature with instruments. Dominic lists reasons against. Janice: "Put it back where we found it - can't have it in house." Mr. Mike yells: "Leave now - they are coming for the creature - not safe here." Crew scrambles with creature downstairs. Dialogue: Conflict about creature. Character: Mr. Mike's warning. Practical: Moving creature downstairs.
19 The Escape Dallas' House - Living Room Day 2 min Act 2 0.5 Doorbell chimes. First life group family arrives - young couple with newborn JP. Introduce themselves to Dallas (acts off). Dominic paces in kitchen. Mr. Mike mutters in chair, looks at worsening weather: "This is all a farce - should be out there returning creature." Couple politely ignores Mr. Mike. Mr. Mike mutters: "45 degrees to the left, 15 to the right." Practical: Doorbell, newborn baby. Character: Life group members. Important: Coordinates repeated.
20 Makeshift Hospital Dallas' House - Living Room/Kitchen Day 3 min Act 2 1 More families arrive. Fill kitchen and living room. Try toasted Brussels sprouts. Janice finds cups for warm apple cider. Dallas excuses to basement for drinks - really checks creature status. Creature barely breathing. Dallas finds shoebox with wife's pictures, puts them aside, puts creature inside. Creature barely breathing, wings curl tightly. Makayla and Asher bring tiny water bottle. Dallas pokes hole to make drinkable. Makayla and Asher watch from staircase. Extras: Life group families. Practical: Brussels sprouts, apple cider, cups, shoebox, wife's pictures, water bottle. Props: Creature in box.
21 Life Group Arrives Dallas' House - Living Room Evening 3 min Act 2 1 Dallas returns to life group. Leader Josh (young couple) gives opening ice breaker. Dallas, Dominic, kids sit silent. Tyler hands out music, everyone sings. Final notes of hymn - hum begins again. People look at each other, comment. Pat (weatherman, life group member) comments on weird weather. Practical: Sheet music, singing. Sound: Hymn, hum interruption. Character: Josh, Tyler, Pat.
22 Basement Huddle Dallas' House - Living Room Evening 2 min Act 2 0.5 Time of reflection. Hum starts again - everyone unsettled. "What the heck was that?" More talk of weird weather patterns plaguing Branford. People leave, hug goodbye. Dominic waves goodbye, waits for last person to leave, then rushes downstairs with kids to see creature Dallas has been nursing. Sound: Hum. Dialogue: Weather discussion. Practical: Goodbyes, hugs.
23 The Hymn Dallas' House - Basement Evening 2 min Act 2 0.5 Creature in makeshift box, heat lamp. Dallas curious - creature responds to machines like little bird. Asher notices small objects (pebbles) arranged in pattern - but when they look back, creature is sleeping. Decision: Drop creature off where found in morning. Dallas stares blankly, half listening. Hum is back. Dominic, Janice, kids, Mr. Mike leave. Mr. Mike: "Mistake to not return creature tonight." Practical: Heat lamp, makeshift box, pebbles pattern. Props: Creature. Visual: Pattern arrangement mystery.
24 Red Eyes Dallas' House - Interior Night 2 min Act 2 0.5 Dallas stares awake at tall window. Through backyard, notices bright eyes watching house from border of tall salt marsh. Looks deeper - eyes fade away. Goes downstairs - creature is gone. Nervous, looks out window again - eyes gone, night still. Visual effects: Bright eyes in distance. Lighting: Night interior/exterior. Props: Creature missing.
25 Creature Missing Dallas' House - Exterior Morning 3 min Act 3 1 Makayla drops off Howie. Notices weird burn marks on grass near driveway - trail leads to windows, then burn mark on side of house. Can't find Dallas - he's staring at abyss outside with tiny detector. Makayla watches, then copies him. They begin to make fence around house. Dallas acknowledges her. Makayla: "What happened?" Dallas: "Creature is gone. But it was injured." Together hear distant cry (creature's), then familiar hum. Makayla: "We have to go get it back - they want to kill it." Practical: Burn marks (SFX/props), fence materials, detector. Visual: Burn trail. Sound: Distant cry, hum.
26 Marsh Confrontation Tall Grass - Salt Marsh Day 6 min Act 3 2.5 Dallas and Makayla walk trail with Howie. See same glow. Rush over. Inch closer to salt marsh - find group of creatures attending to injured creature. At first, younger creatures (2-3) scatter. Older ones wave hands - Dallas and Makayla frozen. Can move heads but not bodies/hands. More lights appear - dozens of glows rise like lanterns in distant mist. Predators emerge - glowing red eyes, shadows go up tall grass as they creep. Paralysis caused by elder creatures' full might, but no match. Focus on predators' actions. MAJOR VFX SEQUENCE. Multiple creatures, glows, predators with red eyes. Practical: Freeze effect (actors). Visual effects: Lantern-like glows, shadows, mist.
27 Predator Attack Tall Grass - Salt Marsh Day 3 min Act 3 1 Predators' eyes blink - causes great fear, creatures huddle. Dallas and Makayla move slowly away, walk briskly back. Dallas has one more interaction with tiny creature - its pulse blinks large, then diminishes. Awful sound of gnashing. Dallas takes out dial - causes creature to pulse again, creatures retract. Dallas quickly grabs creature, runs out. Visual effects: Predator eye blinks, creature pulse, gnashing sound. Props: Dial. Action: Grab and run.
28 The Perimeter Tall Grass - Edge Day 5 min Act 3 2.5 Dallas runs, stops. Realizes creature has stopped pulsing, now motionless. Puts creature down. Other pulses gather. Hearing snarls, Dallas makes way out of tall grass. Crew watches in distance - witness battle of pulse and shadow. Red-eyed creatures surround small creatures, devour them. Mirage of pulsing flickering yellow lights contrasts with red. Then all stops. Bad creatures seek out humans watching. Crew runs out of tall grass, back home. MAJOR VFX BATTLE. Pulse vs shadow battle, creature devouring, yellow vs red lights. Action: Running escape.
29 Dallas' House - Exterior Day 2 min Act 2 0.5 Dallas sits silently contemplating. Without debate, goes outside to dig poles. Takes copper wire, hangs around poles. Checks equipment to ensure in tune. Storm comes. Makayla runs toward mom. Mr. Mike, Asher, Dominic outside. Dallas looks stunned. Continues getting poles, hanging them. Dominic (not saying anything) begins to help. Dallas examines perimeter. News van on radio - Pat Clendenen welcomes Branford to beautiful morning. Practical: Poles, copper wire, digging equipment. Props: Equipment for tuning. Sound: Radio/news van. Weather: Storm approach.
30 Dallas' House - Exterior Day 2 min Act 2 0.5 Dallas jams last pole into mud. Crew stare at tall grass. "I say leave the creature." Dallas and Makayla dig. Hear sudden crawl of fire engines, sea birds, eventually hum. Dallas walks back to house, crew behind. Await on porch, wait for perimeter. Dominic: "This is crazy - what are we doing here?" Sound: Fire engines, sea birds, hum. Practical: Final pole, digging. Location: Porch.
31 Dallas' House - Interior Evening 3 min Act 2 1 Sun sets, crew peer outside. Dominic suggests dinner. Janice agrees, starts cooking pasta. Mr. Mike agrees good time to eat. They eat in silence. Outside, noise begins to grow - same sounds of sniffs (creatures). Makayla rattles in chair impatiently. Makayla breaks ice: "What should we do?" Dominic looks defeated. Dallas remains silent. Mr. Mike rumbles in chair, mutters: "45 degrees to the left, 15 to the right." Decision: Go back. Practical: Pasta cooking, dinner. Sound: Growing noise, sniffs. Dialogue: Decision-making. Important: Coordinates again.
32 Tall Grass Evening 2 min Act 2 1 They go back, see creature again, try to grab it. Eyes follow them. Dominic hesitant, asks Dallas for answers about field. Dominic declines, argues focus on boarding up house for storm. As he speaks, Makayla runs out door, straight into tall grass. Dominic chases but can't reach in time, hesitates at tall grass edge. Cries her name. Asher and Dallas approach tall grass, stare blankly. Visual effects: Eyes following. Action: Makayla runs, chase. Practical: Boarding up materials (mentioned but not done).
33 Dallas' House - Exterior Evening 2 min Act 2 0.5 Dallas begins to power on makeshift poles around edge of tall grass. Howie cowers next to him. Mr. Mike watches from window. Dominic rushes past for flashlights. Calls Makayla on two-way radio - dismayed discovers Asher has other walkie. Dominic asks Dallas to watch Mr. Mike and Howie. Hesitates, then makes out for grass. Practical: Powering poles, flashlights, two-way radios. Props: Equipment. Animal handler: Howie.
34 Dallas' House - Porch/Backyard Evening 3 min Act 2 1 Dallas waits with Howie on porch, watches equipment from afar. Notices little blinking orange light turns green, hurries to inspect. Indicator light: green, then orange pulses. Storm approaches, two get restless, pace around backyard. Mr. Mike inside hears thunder, yells loudly: "They are coming! They are coming!" Dallas looks at makeshift fence, begins to secure it more. Distant cry heard, followed by persistent gradual low hum. Night falls, winds pick up, eyes in fields begin to circulate. Dallas brings Howie inside, heads out to tall grass himself. Practical: Equipment indicator lights, fence securing. Sound: Thunder, cry, hum, wind. Visual effects: Eyes circulating. Weather: Storm, night fall.
35 Tall Grass Night 3 min Act 2 1 Dallas walks into tall grass. Strange silence engulfs him despite whipping winds outside. Sees glimmer of fading light, navigates toward it. Sensation of being watched and followed overwhelms, but presses forward. Dallas stops, pulls crumpled napkin from pocket. Opens it - sees coordinates: 45 L 15 R - matches equipment and little compass on equipment. Props: Crumpled napkin, coordinates, equipment compass. Visual: Fading light. Sound: Silence vs wind contrast. Camera: Subjective POV, being watched feeling.
36 Tall Grass - Hollow Night 4 min Act 3 1.5 In distance sees Makayla holding one of creatures, surrounded by dazed Asher and Dominic sitting next to her. Makayla's eyes fading as Dallas approaches. In Makayla's hands, creature begins to make calls. Little by little, dark eyes around. As creature calls, another creature of same size emerges, begins to nurture. Visual effects: Makayla's fading eyes, dark eyes, creature emergence. Sound: Creature calls. Practical: Multiple creatures.
37 Tall Grass - Hollow Night 7 min Act 3 3 Dallas makes contact with creature. Creature begins to sing a song. Crew paralyzed - including dark forces which cower away. Storm and creatures go back into little holes in gnarls of soft trees. CLIMAX. Sound: Creature song. Visual effects: Paralysis, dark forces cower, creatures retreat. Practical: Multiple creatures, crew.
38 Dallas' House - Interior Night 2 min Act 3 0.5 Dallas, Dominic, Makayla sit by window, watch for night to end. It does. Morning: They wake up. Dallas still making calculations, but then lets it go by putting equipment in trash. Time passage: Night to morning. Props: Equipment in trash. Character: Dallas letting go.
39 Dallas' House - Exterior Morning 1 min Act 3 0.5 Crew leaves, Dallas alone again. Looks outside - hears soft sound pulse of creature. Sound: Soft creature pulse. Character: Dallas alone, connection remains.
40 Town - Various Day 2 min Act 3 0.5 Town back to normal, daily routines. Pat Clendenen reports "miracle of a save" - halfway through storm seemed to counter-direct. Behind Pat - faint red-pulsing eye emerges from background fog - if only for a moment. Location: Town, news van. Visual effects: Red eye in fog (subtle). Practical: Normal town activity, news report.
41 CPC Church - Fellowship Hall Day 2 min Act 3 0.5 Dominic and Dallas greet at church event, don't talk much. Sit next to each other, eat Nica's catered lunch, still don't talk too much. Watch Asher and Makayla observe tiny caterpillar near playground. Makayla puts it in a jar. Practical: Catering (Nica's), caterpillar, jar. Character: Quiet understanding between Dallas and Dominic.
42 Dallas' House - Interior/Exterior Day 3 min Act 3 1 Makayla and Asher busy building little devices of their own. Think this is just start of creature adventure. Dallas still at home, hears echoes in tall grass - something beyond shadows he saw before. Janice drops off Howie again. Dallas greets him. Two watch sun set. Dallas walks to picture of wife, then to Howie, then to tall grass. Dallas turns off equipment, begins to live with salt breeze from marsh. Props: Devices Makayla/Asher build, equipment, wife's picture. Sound: Echoes in tall grass, salt breeze. Visual: Sunset. Character: Dallas accepting new life, letting go of equipment.

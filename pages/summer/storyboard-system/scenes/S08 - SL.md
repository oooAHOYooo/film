## 3D Asset List (For Storyboarder)

### Characters
- **Dallas**: Human male, casual fall/winter clothing.
- **Howie**: Quadruped, small-to-medium dog/creature.

### Environment & Sets
- **Dallas's House Exterior**: Porch, driveway/yard.
- **Branford Rocks**: Rocky coastline, crashing waves, edge of boardwalk.
- **Dirt Path**: Transition area from neighborhood to wild nature.
- **The Marsh**: Dense, tall golden-yellow grasses/reeds (taller than a person).
- **Creek's Edge**: A small creek boundary with a single, standalone tree.

### Props & Equipment
- **Dog Leash**: For Howie.
- **Portable Oscillator**: Handheld device with a prominent fluttering needle.
- **Audio Recorder**: Handheld unit with dials and buttons.
- **Handheld Scope**: A device with a flip-open screen displaying waveforms.
- **Makeshift Trap**: A crude animal trap placed on the ground.
- **Trailcam**: A boxy camera mounted above the trap.
- **Solar Pack & Wires**: A power unit connected to the trailcam with visible wiring.

## Scene 10: The Chase
- Setting: EXT. TALL GRASS / EXT. BRANFORD MONTAGE / EXT. DALLAS DRIVEWAY - NIGHT
- Beat: Dallas flees the marsh with the injured creature as a massive unseen force pursues him back to his home.

### Shot 1 - WIDE - Emerging from the Grass
- Size: Wide
- Camera: Tracking backward
- Action: Dallas hurries out of the dense tall grass. He glances over his shoulder, sensing something is following him.
- Caption: Dallas rushes out of the tall grass.

### Shot 2 - MEDIUM WIDE - The Howl Returns
- Size: Medium Wide
- Camera: Static
- Action: The deep hum returns faintly. In the distance, a chorus of coyotes begins to howl loudly into the night.
- Caption: Coyotes howl as the hum returns.

### Shot 3 - POV WIDE - The Crushing Sound
- Size: POV Wide
- Camera: Handheld, shaking
- Action: Dallas looks back at the dense wall of reeds as a heavy, crushing sound slices through them—something massive rocketing his way.
- Caption: Something massive tears through the reeds.

### Shot 4 - MEDIUM - Breaking into a Sprint
- Size: Medium
- Camera: Tracking fast
- Action: Dallas turns back around and breaks into a full, desperate sprint, holding his jacket tight with his arm.
- Caption: Dallas sprints away from the reeds.

### Shot 5 - WIDE - Panicking Howie
- Size: Wide
- Camera: Static
- Action: Dallas spots the stunted cedar tree. Howie is tied to it, a blur of panic, barking furiously at the tall grass.
- Caption: Howie barks frantically at the encroaching grass.

### Shot 6 - MEDIUM WIDE - Grabbing the Leash
- Size: Medium Wide
- Camera: Handheld, chaotic
- Action: Dallas reaches the tree, snatches Howie's leash, and keeps running without missing a beat, leaving the marsh far behind.
- Caption: Dallas grabs Howie's leash and runs.

### Shot 7 - EXTREME WIDE - Branford Montage
- Size: Extreme Wide
- Camera: Tracking profile, fast
- Action: A brisk, frantic montage of Dallas walking/running rapidly down the same neighborhood route he took earlier, now under the cover of night.
- Caption: Dallas hurries back through his neighborhood.

### Shot 8 - WIDE - The Driveway
- Size: Wide
- Camera: Static
- Action: Dallas finally reaches the edge of his driveway. He stops, doubled over, gasping heavily for air.
- Caption: Dallas stops at his driveway to catch his breath.

### Shot 9 - CLOSE UP - The Fading Pulse
- Size: Close Up
- Camera: Static
- Action: Dallas peers into his coat. The creature is motionless, but its neck emits a very weak, fading amber pulse of light.
- Caption: The creature emits a weak amber pulse inside his coat.

## 3D Asset List (For Storyboarder)

### Characters
- **Dallas**: Human male, casual fall/winter clothing (jacket).
- **Howie**: Quadruped, small-to-medium dog/creature.
- **The Creature**: Small owl/cottonball-like animal, glowing amber.

### Environment & Sets
- **The Marsh / Edge of the Reeds**: Tall dense grasses bordering regular ground.
- **Creek's Edge (The Cedar Tree)**: A standalone stunted cedar tree.
- **Neighborhood Route**: Suburban street, streetlights, pavement.
- **Dallas's Driveway**: The exterior pavement/yard of his house.

### Props & Equipment
- **Dog Leash**: Tied to the tree, held by Dallas.

# S24 - SL

## I. EXT. CENTER OF THE MARSH - LATER

### Shot A (Setup: Reaching the Sanctuary)
- A-1: [Wide]
  - Makayla breaches through a thick wall of reeds and stops. The howling wind suddenly dies down.
- A-2: [Medium Wide]
  - Dallas, Dominic, and Asher break through behind her, collapsing to their knees in the mud under the weight of the speakers.
- A-3: [Extreme Wide]
  - A perfect, geometric 50-foot ring of flattened grass. The tall stalks form an impenetrable woven wall, cutting them off from the storm raging above.

### Shot B (Setup: The Golden Elders)
- B-1: [Wide]
  - The center of the ring is glowing. The Golden Elders sit in a circle in the center.
- B-2: [Medium]
  - The Elders are taller, covered in iridescent golden fur, humming a deep multi-chord frequency.
- B-3: [Low Angle Wide]
  - The frequency visibly vibrates the air, creating a faint, shimmering golden dome over the sanctuary.
- B-4: [Close Up]
  - The weak, erratic pulsing glow in the infant creature's neck flares bright gold as it hears the song.

### Shot C (Setup: Returning the infant)
- C-1: [Medium]
  - Asher gently sets the small creature down in the mud. It stumbles forward, chirping.
- C-2: [Medium Wide]
  - One giant Elder turns its head, sees the baby, and approaches with deliberate grace.
- C-3: [Medium Close Up]
  - The Elder bows its head at Asher and Dallas, emitting a single, isolated acoustic note that rattles Dallas's ribs.

### Shot D (Setup: The Siege begins)
- D-1: [Close Up]
  - Makayla looks at her screen. Jagged red lines track the shadows spiking massively at the edge of the circle.
- D-2: [Wide]
  - A heavy, crushing sound slams into the woven wall of grass on the edge of the circle.
- D-3: [Action Wide]
  - Dozens of massive, corrupted Red-Eye shadows burst through the perimeter. Black rot spreads across the sanctuary floor instantly.

### Shot E (Setup: Clash of Light and Shadow)
- E-1: [Action Medium]
  - The Elders rise to their feet, roaring acoustic blasts of golden light. Fine golden mist sprays from their backs into the air.
- E-2: [Action Tracking]
  - The golden mist meets the black shadows in violent, ozone-scented snaps. The Red-Eyes swarm like ghouls devouring the light.
- E-3: [Medium Wide]
  - The massive Red-Eyes swarm the largest Golden Elder, ripping into its acoustic field with jagged static, pushing it back to shield the injured creature.

### Shot F (Setup: Fighting Back)
- F-1: [Medium Close Up]
  - Dominic screams, an animalistic roar of adrenaline and fear, heaving the heavy speaker off the ground.
- F-2: [Action Wide]
  - Dallas grabs the other speaker. They wade through the chaotic crossfire of light and shadow.
- F-3: [Medium Wide]
  - They position the two massive wooden frames in a V-shape in the very center of the ring.

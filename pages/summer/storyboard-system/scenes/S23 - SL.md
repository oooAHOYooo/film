# S23 - SL

## I. EXT. TALL GRASS - CONTINUOUS

### Shot A (Setup: Enter the tall grass)
- A-1: [Wide Tracking]
  - Dallas and Asher enter the tall grass carrying the heavy equipment.
- A-2: [Medium]
  - The nylon straps bite into Dallas' shoulders, straining the fabric.
- A-3: [Close Up]
  - Dallas drops the speaker into the mud in pain. He looks at his hand—black veins pulse under his skin, beginning to look translucent.

### Shot B (Setup: Dominic arrives)
- B-1: [Medium Wide]
  - Dominic runs towards Dallas and Asher, screaming for Makayla.
- B-2: [Medium]
  - Dominic paces and takes a hit from his inhaler.
- B-3: [Medium Close Up]
  - Dallas grips his arm tightly and stoically marches onward. Dominic takes another hit.

### Shot C (Setup: Sensory Deprivation)
- C-1: [POV]
  - Crossing the threshold into pitch black. The tall grass towers over ten feet high, whipping violently in unpredictable rhythms.
- C-2: [Wide Tracking]
  - Makayla sweeps the handheld speaker like a flashlight, the small blinking green LED on her chest rig leading the way.
- C-3: [Medium Tracking]
  - Asher follows, hunched low with the backpack containing the golden creature. Dallas and Dominic take up the rear, sinking deep into the marsh mud.

### Shot D (Setup: The Red-Eye Above)
- D-1: [Low Angle Wide]
  - Massive, formless shadows drift lazily through the storm clouds. Hundreds of glowing red eyes pierce the dark.
- D-2: [Wide]
  - A massive shadow drops down fifty feet ahead. The concussive force knocks Dominic to his knees.
- D-3: [Medium Close Up]
  - Dominic groans, his heavy wooden speaker sliding dangerously on his shoulder.
- D-4: [Medium]
  - Makayla freezes as the Red-Eye thrashes through the reeds in their path. The black rot spreads toward Dominic's boot.

### Shot E (Setup: Asher's Distraction)
- E-1: [Over the Shoulder]
  - The Red-Eye stops, its massive red eyes scanning the area. The silence stretches.
- E-2: [Medium Close Up]
  - Asher steps forward, closes his eyes, taps his leg twice, and begins to hum a mathematically perfect, off-key frequency.
- E-3: [Wide]
  - The Red-Eye snaps its head in the opposite direction and drifts off, chasing the ghost sound.

### Shot F (Setup: Moving Forward)
- F-1: [Medium]
  - Makayla exhales slowly. Asher points forward toward the center.
- F-2: [Medium Wide]
  - Dominic pulls his boot out of the thick mud. Dallas helps him up. They push deeper into the howling void.

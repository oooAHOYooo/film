Story & Intent

Creatures in the Tall Grass is an atmospheric exploration of isolation, memory, and the uncanny. Set against the backdrop of a remote wilderness location, the film follows a protagonist whose perception of reality begins to fracture as they confront both external threats and internal demons.

The visual language draws from natural textures, shifting light, and the tension between vast landscapes and claustrophobic interiors. The narrative structure weaves together past and present, blurring the lines between what is remembered, what is imagined, and what is truly happening.

Through careful attention to sound design, cinematography, and performance, the film aims to create an immersive experience that challenges the audience's own sense of perception and reality.

Story & Intent

Dallas moves to the coastal town of Branford, Connecticut, carrying military-grade audio equipment and the weight of loss. As he settles into his new home near the salt marshes, he begins to detect a mysterious hum—a frequency that seems to emanate from the tall grass beyond his backyard. Drawn by strange readings and an otherworldly light, Dallas discovers a fragile, wounded creature hidden in the marsh, setting in motion an extraordinary chain of events.

As an approaching Nor'easter threatens the coast, Dallas finds himself at the center of an unexpected community—his neighbor Dominic and his family, a church life group, and the enigmatic Mr. Mike, who speaks in cryptic coordinates. Together, they must protect the creature from predatory forces while navigating the boundary between the known world and something ancient that exists just beyond perception.

Creatures in the Tall Grass is a story about grief, connection, and the choice to protect something vulnerable in the face of overwhelming danger. Set against the atmospheric backdrop of New England's coastal marshes, the film explores how loss can open us to unexpected forms of kinship—with each other, with the natural world, and with forces we barely understand.

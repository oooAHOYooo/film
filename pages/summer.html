Story & Intent

Dallas relocates to Branford, Connecticut to start a bioacoustics job recording the marsh (birds, insects, coyotes). After unpacking, he hears a low hum that his equipment can’t fully capture.

He meets his neighbor Dominic and is pulled into a church lunch, family dynamics, and the arrival of Dominic’s sister Janice and her kids (Makayla and Asher). Mr. Mike fixates on the weather and scratches coordinates (45 left, 15 right) before Dominic flips the table to stop him.

As a Nor’easter approaches, Dallas’s readings spike near the tall grass. He follows a signal into the reeds and finds a small creature and a crystal. Bringing it home triggers a larger response: the hum escalates, the house reacts, and predatory red eyes close in. The group attempts to protect the creature and hold the perimeter as the storm hits.

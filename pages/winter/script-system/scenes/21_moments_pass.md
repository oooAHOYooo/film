EXT. OUTPOST — DAY

The two begin to walk toward the thicket of the bush again.

Henri is left behind—feeling as if he just denied the birthday boy his final wish before his death. Beating himself up in the process.

No, he tells himself. This is all absurd. Don't feel bad for these crazy folks. They don't know the laws of physics. They live in la la land.

Henri, to himself: Lord, if you are there—get me out of this hell.

INT. DETECTIVE OFFICE

Rob and Max emerge from Rob's office, geared up and ready to take on The Weevil. They walk with purpose through the detective office, catching the attention of their fellow colleagues. DETECTIVE BOSS, a stern and authoritative figure, steps forward, blocking their path.

DETECTIVE BOSS
(firmly)
What the hell do you two think you're doing? I thought I made it clear that you were off this case!

ROB
(defiantly)
We have evidence, sir. We know where The Weevil's hiding.

DETECTIVE BOSS
Oh you do?

ROB
Yes.

DETECTIVE MILLER
And where's that, Nibbler?

DETECTIVE BOSS
(gesturing for Miller to shut up)
Let's have some respect for the Nibbler boys — he is the town's hero — but for what

THE GHOST
Not now sir.

DETECTIVE BOSS
(in a mocking tone)
You're right Max — not now — okay Rob — go out and find the Weevil.

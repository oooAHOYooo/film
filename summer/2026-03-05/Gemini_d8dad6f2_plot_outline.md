# Full Script Plot Outline (28 Scenes)

This is the complete, start-to-finish narrative arc of the script, incorporating the newly expanded "Fight to the Marsh" climax. 

## Act 1: Arrival & Discovery
**Status Quo and the Mystery of the Noise**
*   **Scene 1-2 (The Arrival):** Dallas moves into his new home next to the tall grass. The first night, his sleep is disturbed by strange flickering lights in the marsh and a bizarre hum that drains his equipment's batteries when he opens his late wife Sierra's box. 
*   **Scene 3-7 (The Neighbors & The Mystery):** Dallas meets Makayla and Asher, who are immediately interested in his military-grade oscillator. Strange events plague the neighborhood—Pat Clendenen reports from news vans, and mysterious burn marks appear on Dominic's driveway. Asher and Makayla grab coordinates from Mr. Mike's scratching.
*   **Scene 8-11 (First Contact):** Dallas ventures into the marsh following the hum and his equipment. He discovers an injured, bioluminescent golden creature, but immediately realizes something much darker (the shadows) is hunting it. He rescues the golden creature and brings it back to his house.

## Act 2: The Creature
**Studying the Golden Frequency and the Shadow Threat**
*   **Scene 12-14 (The Bond):** Dallas, Makayla, and Asher study the creature in secret. They realize it communicates via frequencies. It sings, and Makayla feels a deep connection to its power. 
*   **Scene 15-17 (The Reverse Engineering):** They realize the burn marks are left by the hunting shadows (Red-Eyes). Dallas uses Mr. Mike's numbers to calibrate his oscillator to the creature's exact frequency (115.3 MHz), realizing it can be used as a weapon to cancel out the shadows.
*   **Scene 18-20 (The Secret is Out):** Dominic hosts LifeGroup at Dallas’s house. The creature's presence is accidentally revealed. Dominic discovers Sierra’s old research folder, realizing these "ghost stories" are real and have been localized here for decades.
*   **Scene 21 (The Swarm):** The neighborhood loses power as a massive army of Red-Eyes besieges Dallas's house. Makayla brilliantly uses a portable speaker to test the 115.3 MHz frequency on a burn mark, reversing the black rot and proving the phase-canceler works. But they are completely surrounded.

## Act 3: The Climax (The Return)
**The Fight to the Marsh**
*   **Scene 22 (The Tactical Load-Out):** The house is breached. Realizing that firing the massive acoustic shockwave inside the living room will destroy the house and kill them, Dallas, Dominic, Makayla, and Asher strap the heavy speakers and car batteries to their backs and escape out the back door into the terrifying marsh.
*   **Scene 23 (The Trench Run):** The group endures a grueling, pitch-black push through the tall grass. They have to use Makayla's portable speaker and Asher's uncanny ability to mimic the frequency to stealthily avoid massive, patrolling Red-Eye shadows while carrying eighty-pound equipment through the mud. 
*   **Scene 24 (The Sanctuary & The Siege):** They discover the "eye of the storm"—a perfectly flattened ring of grass where the giant Golden Elders are hiding, using their own frequency to block the storm. They reunite the injured baby creature, but the Red-Eyes launch a massive, coordinated attack, overwhelming the Elders' natural defenses.
*   **Scene 25 (The Final Blast):** In the mud and chaos, Makayla frantically wires the trap. The Red-Eyes charge. Dominic sacrifices his pristine nature to sink into the mud, using his body weight to hold the heavy speakers upright. Dallas triggers the oscillator, sending a concussive blast of golden light through the marsh that phase-cancels the shadows, completely destroying them and saving the Elders.

## Act 4: Aftermath
**Resolution and Full Circle**
*   **Scene 26-27 (Peace Restored):** The crew rests at Dallas's apartment. The town of Branford goes back to normal, oblivious to the war that just occurred in the grass. A changed Dominic enjoys the quiet of a church picnic. Dallas walks into the now-peaceful marsh and retrieves a perfectly clean recording of the Elders from his damaged equipment.
*   **Scene 28 (Full Circle):** Makayla and Asher set up a permanent, upgraded listening station in the woods, embracing their roles as protectors/listeners. Out by the creek, Dallas plays the pure recording of the golden hum and realizes the rhythm perfectly matches the cadence of his late wife Sierra's breathing—proving she was deeply connected to their frequency all along.

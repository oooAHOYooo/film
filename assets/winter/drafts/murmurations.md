# Murmurations

It means that they are coming soon. “What do you mean – they?” There is only one night wolf. Yes. That might be true – that is what grandfather says – but you know as well as I do that there are more than one. If not, why put up so much security.

The brothers take water to their mother who is in another hut. The small village’s creek fills into the bucket as the snow melts in the distance. And as this happens the roar of the towns alarm begins. And everyone begins to hunker for shelter.

Just then a giant shadowy creature emerges. It’s shadow engulfs the little village huts. As it approaches it is met with hundreds of tiny lights. And within a moment the arrows begin to fly. It waves it off and it begins to march back.

The brothers find their mother at their cousins house huddled up by the bed with the rest of their family. Without hestitation they grab the swords that are on top of the fire place and cautiously approach the window. A younger kid in fustration cries out “Those swords are not going to do anything” – “Stop that nonsense” went the reply – The Heebelease was taken down by such swords “How can?” – the Heebee don’t like it. Quiet everyone – says the mother.
